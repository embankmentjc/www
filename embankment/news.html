NJ SHPO Issues Opinions on Harsimus Branch and Other Historic Assets Along the Branch

After considerable study, the Surface Transportation Board (STB) Office of Environmental Analysis (OEA) and New Jersey State Historic Preservation Office (SHPO) agreed on the historic sites within the Area of Potential Effects of a proposed Harsimus Branch abandonment. Most notable among new SHPO-issued Opinions is that the entire Pennsylvania Railroad (PRR) Harsimus Branch right of way, from Marin Boulevard to Waldo Avenue, is eligible for the National Register of Historic Places. Persuasive field research on the ROW was conducted by Jersey City historic preservation officers and Embankment Coalition volunteers and historic preservation experts.

The complete list of sites and historic districts that the OEA and SHPO agreed would be affected by abandonment are discussed in Cultural Resources Identification Report Addendum. Harsimus Branch Abandonment.
